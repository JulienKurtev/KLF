Our Story

KLF began way back in 2005 with the launch of gameaccess.ca, an online, subscription-based video game rental service.

The first subscription rental site in Canada.

KLF Media Inc. started with the release of its first online business, GameAccess.ca, a subscription-based video game rental service. Game Access was the first website in Canada to provide simple, web-based, video game rentals. The business rose to prominence rapidly and has since become Canada’s leader in online video game rentals, offering the largest collection of console video game of any rental service - on or off line!
